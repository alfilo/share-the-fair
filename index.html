Goals of the Share the Fair project

It would be wonderful for both the youth and the community for us to show how the 4-Hers, even through the pandemic, have thrived and persevered, continuing to develop their leadership skills, strengthening the bond between 4-H and their community, committing to personal growth, and doing so many wonderful projects and activities this last year. The New York State 4-H Mission statement states: 4-H connects youth to hands-on learning opportunities that help them grow into competent, caring, contributing members of society. Their vision is a world in which youth and adults learn, grow and work together as catalysts for positive change. The Share the Fair website enables us to work together to make this positive change happen. By joining together, people all over our county can still have a day at the fair.

4-H Pledge

I pledge my head to greater thinking,
My heart to greater loyalty,
My hands to greater service,
And my health for better living
For my club, my community, my country, and my world.
